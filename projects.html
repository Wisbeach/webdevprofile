Project 2: The Curious Humans Network

Description: This is a platform from where you can explore the human experience and discover and book events and activities in your local area in London.

The idea is to promote positive mental health and curiosity by offering interesting ways to be in the present moment while also hopefully connecting with like-minded individuals

One of the special features we built for this platform is a navigation menu with each category assigned a specific colour. When A specific colour is chosen the user will be shown suggestions of activities related to that colour.
